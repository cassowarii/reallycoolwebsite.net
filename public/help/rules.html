Hi. I'll try to keep this brief.
  1. Don't post things that are illegal. (e.g. Internet piracy, etc.)
  2. Don't harass other users.
  3. Don't post and/or link to hateful/racist/homophobic/etc content.
  4. Don't post things that are generally not work-safe (explicit imagery, gore, etc)
  5. Don't post or link to AI-generated content. (content that uses AI or is about is OK, but don't use AI to generate post text or link to primarily AI-written sites for spam purposes, etc)
  6. Don't post purely to advertise your own commercial services.
  7. Don't sign up if you are under the age of 13. This is due to U.S. federal law, so it's serious business.

That's all, thanks. Be careful, it's a scary internet out there these days. I wish you the best of luck in navigating it.
