> privacy policy

This website does not track you or use any type of cookies, other than the necessary session cookies to keep track of which account you are currently logged into. In fact, the website does not even use any Javascript, currently.

However, this is a website where you can upload user-generated content, so please be careful with what you upload. I recommend not uploading personally-identifying information.

An email address is optional at the moment, but will be necessary to reset your password (once I implement this feature.)

Also, as the administrator of the website, I can view anything you upload — even if I someday add privacy settings, anything you post is always viewable by me, so please do not post sensitive information. I do not want to read it.

Lastly, this website does include Youtube embeds, which in accordance with Google policy will try to track you when they load on the page. You can disable these in user settings, to replace them with static images linking to the Youtube page.

If you are under the age of 13, due to various laws and regulations, it is prohibited to sign up for this website.

That's all, thanks. Be careful, it's a scary internet out there these days. I wish you the best of luck in navigating it.
